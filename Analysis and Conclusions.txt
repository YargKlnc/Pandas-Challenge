Conclusions:
-Charter Schools have higher Passing Rates in Mathematics and Reading compared to District Schools
-Students in Charter Schools obtain higher Average Scores compared to District Schools
-Charter Schools Populations are less than 2300 students per school and have Higher Overall Passing Rates compared to District Schools
-Highest Performing Schools are Charter Type Schools
-Budget per student in Charter Schools are lower than District Schools 
-Total School Budgets are higher in District Schools (also due to population of students) compared to Charter Schools
-Schools with Spending per student less than $585 has higher Overall Passing Rates
